About MyScratchBlocks

MyScratchBlocks is an open-source project dedicated to extending the capabilities of Scratch and TurboWarp. Our goal is to provide a powerful yet intuitive set of new programming blocks that empower creators to build even more sophisticated and engaging interactive experiences. We believe in fostering a collaborative environment where users can contribute ideas, report bugs, and help shape the future of the modification.

Developed by a passionate team of developers and educators, MyScratchBlocks aims to bridge the gap between visual programming and advanced computational concepts, making complex ideas accessible to a wider audience. Join us on this exciting journey to push the boundaries of what's possible with Scratch!
